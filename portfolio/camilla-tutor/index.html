About

17 years of turning exam anxiety into academic success

I have worked as an academic tutor for the past 17 years with great success. I teach from 7+ to GCSE level, specialising in 11+ English and Maths, as well as interview preparation. I am a supportive, interactive tutor who aims to inspire all my students to achieve their full academic potential.

I am lucky enough to now be able to tutor full time, around my own childcare, due to high demand. Parents can rest assured that I do not need to fit lessons in around other work. That means total focus on lesson planning and greater flexibility when rearranging sessions.

I conduct up to date research on each target school because what has been tested before is the best indicator of what is to come. I work predominantly with past papers, of which I have a substantial supply, and I ensure my students are more than ready so that nothing in an exam comes as a surprise.

I make lessons as engaging, fun and interactive as possible, using visual aids, creative tools and the child's own ideas, whilst sticking firmly to the curriculum. Many students struggle with unhelpful learning strategies for years. I have tried and tested methods for success.
